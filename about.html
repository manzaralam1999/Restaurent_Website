About Manzar Pakistani Restaurent

Here at Manzar Pakistani Restaurent, we believe that food is a celebration of life and that traditional foods are an enduring part of our culture. Family owned and operated, we challenge ourselves everyday to live up to our name by preparing and serving traditional, Pakistani home style meals with devotion, elegance, and grace. Each of us has special memories of home-made-food - the kind that keeps us longing for it when we are away from home. Capturing this authentic flavor has become a critical undertaking for Manzar Pakistani Restaurent and we are proud to continue to cook traditional meals from recipes perfected from over thousands of years.
A Pakistani food-lover’s dream, Manzar’s food is prepared for you using the finest seasonal ingredients in separate vegetarian and non-vegetarian kitchens by our specially trained chefs. Good food has the ability to delight your sense of taste and leave you happier. Our menu features a large range of vegetables, meats and delicious desserts from Pakistan’s rich culture including authentic Pakistani cuisine that tastes like it came straight from Kerala. Each menu item is specifically tailored with flair and attention to detail is characteristic of Manzar Pakistani Restaurent for every occasion, whether it be a large dinner party, family outing, intimate first date. Regardless of the occasion, you can always expect a great atmosphere with great food at the Manzar Pakistani Restaurent.
